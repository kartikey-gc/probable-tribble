Skechers History

Skechers has its foundation in the United States. Today, Skechers ranks third amongst the US footwear market players. The brand was first introduced by Robert Greenberg in 1992. Focusing on men street shoes initially, Skechers as a brand evolved over the years and gained popularity even for women and kids’ apparel and footwear. Skechers being a global market player is active with various collaborations to represent the line of vibrant lifestyle products strongly. This is evident as Skechers endorsed its performance athlete collection with Meb Keflezighi, silver medallist in marathon running in 2004. Besides, many renowned celebrities and top sportspersons have been associated with Skechers to feature the super collection of Skechers apparel and footwear online. Recently in 2022, Skechers has achieved yet another milestone with sponsoring the first nationally televised pickleball tournament in the U.S.
